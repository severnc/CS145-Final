Our Story

Since 1609 the Carboidrati family has been making authentic Italiano cuisine. Food straight from Italy now located conveniently near downtown Bozeman, Montana! Our menu has stood the test of time and generation after generation has fallen in love with the recipies. We encourage people from all walks of life to join us at our table and enjoy our traditional cuisine.
